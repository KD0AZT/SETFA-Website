Smart911

Smart911 is a confidential service that allows you to create a profile with important information, such as family members, health and mobility issues, door/gate lock information or pets in your home, to 911 dispatchers. They pass pertinent information to first responders in advance.

The information you provide is only visible to dispatchers and first responders.

The service also provides you with the option to receive notifications about emergency situationsthat may affect you.
