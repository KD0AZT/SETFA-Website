In an emergency, CALL 911 immediately. When firefighters are on duty, their responsibility is responding to 911 calls, ensuring a state of operational readiness, training, and conducting life safety inspections.

If no one answers when you call S.E. Thurston Fire (SETFA), please leave a message with your name and phone number (please speak slowly and clearly). When stopping by the station, please ring the doorbell if the front door is locked. If available, someone will answer the door.

Administrative Office Hours: 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday
