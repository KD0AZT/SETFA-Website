BBQ Safety

Below are some safety tips to read before firing up the grill this summer. Any time you work with fire there is a chance of getting burned. Be cautious, use common sense, and follow these helpful safety tips:

  • Grills are for outside use ONLY.
  • Use in a well-ventilated area.
  • Keep grill on a stable surface to prevent tipping
  • Keep the fire under control
  • Be ready to extinguish flames
  • NEVER leave a grill unattended once lit
