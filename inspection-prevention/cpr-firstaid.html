CPR Classes

Classes are held the first Tuesday of every month at S.E. Thurston Fire headquarters: 709 Mill Rd SE, Yelm WA 98597 (360) 458-2799.

  • All Medic One sponsored classes are FREE to the general public and approcimately 3 hours in duration.
  • You must register to attend a CPR class. To register, please visit the Medic One Website.
  • Classes teach One Person Adult CPR
  • All classes are trained to the American Heart Standards of Washington

Hands-Only CPR

Learn about hands-only CPR so that you can be prepared for an emergency sitation. You can help those in need! To find out more, visit the American Heart Association website.

First Aid Classes

Medic One does not provide First Aid training nor Health Care Provider classes. For further questions, please call (360) 704-2790.

Free Blood Pressure Checks

S.E. Thurston Fire offers free blood pressure checks. No appointment is necessary, but please keep in mind that staff and firefighters may be on an emergency call and unavailable to provide blood pressure checks. Call the office to check if anyone is available: (360) 458-2799. If no one answers, please leave a message. Please speak clearly and slowing when leaving a voice mail. Upon arriving, ring the doorbell at the front door for assistance.
